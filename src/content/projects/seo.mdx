**Thulite SEO** is the official SEO integration for Thulite websites, providing essential search engine optimization features with a simple setup and flexible configuration. It includes meta tags, Open Graph and Twitter card support, structured data schemas (e.g., Website, BreadcrumbList, BlogPosting), and automatic favicon generation to help improve your site's visibility and sharing.

Designed to work seamlessly with the Thulite framework, Thulite SEO makes it easy to configure defaults for titles, descriptions, images, and structured data while allowing page-level overrides. Whether you're publishing blogs, docs, or product pages, this integration helps ensure your site follows SEO best practices out of the box.
