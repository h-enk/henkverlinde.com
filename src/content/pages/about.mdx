I'm the Founder & CEO and Digital Innovation Lead at [Ventizo](https://ventizo.com/), where I create practical, future-ready digital solutions that empower developers and modern organizations. With a Master of Science (MSc) in Economics and Business from [Erasmus University Rotterdam](https://www.eur.nl/en), I combine analytical insight, technical expertise, and product vision to turn ideas into impact.

## Background

Before founding Ventizo, I built my career in international banking as a Business Analyst and Business Developer, helping to design and develop global Payments & Cash Management solutions and investment products. This experience shaped my structured, value-driven approach to innovation.

## Ventizo
